About us
SILQ draws its soul from the ancient Silk Route, a passage where Persia, Hindustan, the Levant and the Mediterranean once met through spice, stories and shared traditions.
 Our cuisine is shaped by this heritage, expressed in dishes that are layered, elegant and quietly indulgent. Every plate is crafted with balance in mind, letting depth, nuance and honest ingredients take centre stage.
 The space reflects the same philosophy: warm light, handcrafted details and textures inspired by age-old cultures. At SILQ, luxury is gentle, an atmosphere where you can slow down, savour each course and enjoy hospitality that feels intuitive, graceful and personal.

Experience


At SILQ, the experience begins before the first course arrives. As you take your seat, a Ritual Note awaits you, a gentle guide to the evening’s journey, the stories behind our flavours and the small moments we’ve woven in for you to discover.


The ambience shifts with every visit: soft light, calm textures and live music that changes each day. One evening it may be the sitar, another the rabab, sarangi or sarod, each bringing its own mood and magic to the room. No two nights feel alike and that’s the beauty of it.


At SILQ, you don’t just dine, you experience something that stays with you and keeps you coming back.

At SILQ, dining is a ritual of presence inspired by the Silk Route. Through fragrance, flame, taste, touch and music, tradition meets storytelling, awakening the senses and leaving an enduring impression.
